Info about Italy

The capital city of Italy is Rome.
Italy, officially the Italian Republic, is a country consisting of a continental part.
Italy is located in south-central Europe, and is considered part of western Europe.
Delimited by the Alps and a peninsula surrounded by several islands.
The country covers a total area of 301,340 km (116,350 sq mi)
